Information Collection and Use

This information or any other information displayed in the app will be retained on your device only and is not collected or viewable by me in any way. This application uses credentials you supply to retreive and display grade and class information from your gradebook system server directly. Therefore, I have no access to the information you access through GradeWay.

I want to inform you that whenever you use GradeWay, in the case of a crash in the app, your device will send log data to the Play Store or App Store servers. This Log Data may include information such as device name, operating system version, the time and date of your use, and other non-sensitive statistics.

Cookies

Cookies are files with a small amount of data that are commonly used as anonymous unique identifiers. These are sent to your browser from the websites that you visit and are stored on your device's internal memory.
