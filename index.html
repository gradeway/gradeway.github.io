Details

Only login once to quickly check your grades.

Spend less time getting more information out of your grades. Skip the login and view useful insights about your grades!

  • View your color coded averages for all your courses, by each weight category, and for every marking period.
  • View your class averages over time to see trends and improvements.
  • See how your average has changed between sessions.
  • See what grades were recently added, coming up, and missing.

Use the What If Calculator to predict your average by adding mock grades and changing scores to see how it affects your average. This will help you better set goals for the scores you need in the future.

Plan out your homework and tasks so you can stay on top of school, even if it's online now.

Easily plan your homework out and set reminders for yourself, without needing a notebook or manually entering in courses yourself. The planner works seamlessly with other features and data from HAC.

Add homework assignments and tasks, seamlessly integrated with your HAC courses. View your work assigned by teachers from HAC and your own work, side by side, day by day, all in the same place. Set push notifications to stay on top of your work.

Simulate the bell and keep track of time with a custom bell schedule. See how much time is left in each period and get notified before the next one starts so you are never late. Students in online school will be able to follow the same schedule theyâ€™re used to, at home!

Easily view important info from HAC, with support for multiple students.

Relevant info from HAC simplified and made more accessible:

  • Class schedule separated by A and B day and by semester.
  • Locker number/combination and course requests for the next year.
  • Report cards and progress reports for every date and marking period.
  • Unofficial transcript and credits.
  • Attendance in an easy to understand calendar for each month.

Email your teachers from within the app, without having to know their email addresses yourself. Just select a teacher and write an email.

Add multiple students on the same device and easily switch between them. Extremely useful if you are a parent with multiple students.

Use a versatile and customizable GPA calculator to know your GPA in advance and understand the factors contributing to it.

You no longer have to wait for the end of the semester to know your GPA. UpGrade calculates your weighted and 4.0 GPA using all of your averages, your transcript, and all your credits. View GPA breakdowns, edit scales, and exclude courses to improve accuracy.

View the GPA of each of your courses to know what you are doing well on and what classes you should focus more on raising your score. Compare GPA across marking periods to see if you are improving.
