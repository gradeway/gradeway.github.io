Instructions

Open your district's HAC login page in your browser and find the website URL. It should look something like this: https://hac.friscoisd.org/HomeAccess/Account/LogOn

Copy the part after the "https://" and before the "/HomeAccess". Underlined here: https://hac.friscoisd.org/HomeAccess/Account/LogOn

Now paste it into GradeWay.

If an error occurs when you try to login through GradeWay, but you are entering your login info correctly, please contact me at the support email below.
